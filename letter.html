First of all...

I hope somewhere inside you, the Azira I once knew is still there. Even if it's just a little.

Sayang...

I miss you so, so much. Where have you gone? Are you really not coming back? You know... ever since you left, I've cried a lot. Back then, every time I cried, you were always there to comfort me. Now, I have to comfort myself, and honestly... it doesn't feel the same anymore. I miss how happy I was when you were around. I miss listening to every little story you wanted to tell me. I miss your laugh. I miss the way you treated me. I miss the way you loved me. I even miss the way you got mad at me whenever I did something stupid. I miss how jealous you got because you cared. I miss how excited you were to tell me everything that happened in your day. I miss every little thing about you. More than anything... I miss us.

I know I made mistakes. I know I hurt you. I'm truly sorry. I know "sorry" can't erase what I've done. I know I can't change the past. But I can promise you one thing...

No more lies.

No more hiding.

No more hurting you.

When you asked me about my past... I chose to hide the truth. Not because I was proud of it. Not because I still loved someone else. But because I was afraid. I was terrified that if I told you everything, I'd lose you. I thought hiding the truth would protect our relationship. Instead... It became the very thing that destroyed it. That will always be the biggest regret of my life.

I also made another mistake. I kept talking to someone I should've stopped talking to a long time ago. Even though I never wanted to go back... I still made a stupid decision. And I said things that never came from my heart. No matter what my intention was... Those words hurt you. And for that... I'm truly sorry.

If one day... No matter how small the chance is... You decide to give me another opportunity... I won't waste it. I want to take care of you the way we once dreamed about. I want to grow old with you. I want to build the future we used to imagine together. Not just because I miss you... But because you're still the person I want to spend my life with.

I never wanted to leave you. Not even once. If it was for you... I would've done anything. I never bought flowers for anyone before. But I bought them for you. Not because flowers can erase my mistakes... But because I wanted you to know how much you mean to me. I just wanted to see you smile again. Because your smile was always my favourite.

Can I tell you something honestly? The Azira I see today... Feels so different. Every time you look at me... It feels like there's no warmth left. Maybe I deserve that. Maybe I'm the reason the old Azira slowly disappeared. And honestly... That thought hurts me more than anything else. I miss the Azira I knew. The Azira who trusted me. The Azira who made me laugh. The Azira who always made me feel loved. The Azira who dreamed about our future together. I miss her so much.

Sometimes I get jealous. Not because I don't trust you. But because I know there are so many people who would want someone as amazing as you. And deep inside... I'm scared that one day someone else will make you happier than I ever did. That fear exists because I know... I'm the one who caused all of this.

I don't expect you to forgive me today. I don't expect everything to go back to normal overnight. Because I know trust isn't rebuilt with beautiful words. Trust is rebuilt through actions. If you ever choose to give me another chance... I'll spend every day proving that I've changed. Not because I have to. But because I genuinely want to become someone worthy of your love.

If, after reading all of this... Your answer is still "no"... I'll respect it. Because I understand why. I was the one who broke the trust we built together. I only hope you'll never think that my love for you was fake. Because it never was. The only fake thing... Was the lies I told because I was afraid of losing you. And that became the biggest mistake of my life.

Before I end this letter... There's one last thing I want to say. If one day you've already found someone else... I truly hope he takes care of you better than I ever did. I hope he protects your heart. I hope he respects your feelings. I hope he never gives you a reason to cry the way I did. Because that's what you deserve. It would break my heart to know you're no longer mine. But if he's the person who can give you the happiness that I failed to protect... Then I sincerely hope he never lets you go. As for me... I'll slowly learn to stop hoping. I'll slowly stop disturbing your life. Not because I stopped loving you. But because I don't want my love to become another burden for you. If letting you go is what truly makes you happy... Then I'll learn to accept it. Even if it breaks me.

I still love you. Not because I'm lonely. Not because I need someone to fill the emptiness. But because... Out of everyone who has ever walked into my life... I still choose Azira Ashiqin. And if somewhere inside your heart... The Azira I once knew is still there... Even if it's only a tiny part of her... I hope she knows... That I'm still here. Still regretting. Still praying. Still hoping. Not to erase the past... But for one chance to create a better future than the one I ruined.

I Love You.

❤️

Always.
